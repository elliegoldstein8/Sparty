SPARTY BROS





Super Sparty Bros is a game requirement by the course Game Development for Modern Platforms authorized by Michigan State University at Coursera. Sparty is wearing an armor that most knights wear during a battle. Help Sparty get away from Enemies, stun them, collect coins, and get the ROSE to win the game!
